## What We Are Not

To be explicit, ReferrAI is **not**:

- A company or commercial product
- An advertising network or affiliate platform
- A tracking or identity solution
- A payments or revenue distribution system

These are intentional boundaries.

---

## Governance & Participation

ReferrAI is community-driven and open by design.

- Discussions happen in public GitHub issues and PRs
- Contributions are reviewed with a strong **Non-Goals** posture
- Long-term stewardship may transition to a neutral standards body

We welcome participation from:
- AI platform teams
- Retailers and retail media networks
- Publishers and content platforms
- Privacy, policy, and standards experts
